campuslog

School RFID Attendance & Chat System

Privacy Policy
📋

Overview

This privacy policy applies to the campuslog app for mobile devices, created by Ritabrata Biswas as an Ad Supported service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

📊

Information we collect

  • Your device's Internet Protocol (IP) address
  • Your phone number, collected via Firebase Authentication for user verification and login only
  • Pages visited within the app, time and date of visits
  • Time spent on the application
  • Operating system of your mobile device
We do not gather precise location data from your mobile device.
📷

Camera permission

The app requests access to your device camera solely for scanning RFID/QR codes to record student attendance.

No images or videos are ever captured, stored, or shared. The camera is only active during the scanning process.
🔔

Push notifications

The app sends push notifications to inform users about attendance updates and chat messages. These are essential to the core functionality of the system.

You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

💬

Chat system & encryption

campuslog includes an in-app chat system for school communication. All chat messages are encrypted to ensure the privacy and security of all school communications.

Chat data is only accessible to authorized users within the school system.

🔗

Third party services

Google Play Services
AdMob
Firebase Analytics
Firebase Crashlytics
Firebase Authentication
Firebase Cloud Messaging

Data disclosure

We may disclose your information only when:

  • Required by law or legal process
  • Necessary to protect rights, safety, or investigate fraud
  • Shared with trusted service providers bound by this policy
🚪

Opt-out rights

You can stop all data collection by uninstalling the app using the standard uninstall process on your mobile device or via the Google Play Store.

🗂️

Data retention

We retain your data for as long as you use the app and for a reasonable time after. To request deletion of your data, contact us at work.dev.rik@gmail.com.

👶

Children's privacy

This app does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided personal information, it will be immediately deleted. Parents or guardians may contact us at work.dev.rik@gmail.com.

🔒

Security

We provide physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect your information. All in-app chat messages are encrypted to protect user privacy.
