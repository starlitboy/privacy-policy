About this policy

This privacy policy applies to the campuslog mobile application, created by Ritabrata Biswas as an ad-supported service. This service is provided "AS IS". By using the application, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

manage_accounts
Information we collect
  • Device IP address
  • Phone number — collected via Firebase Authentication solely for account verification and secure login
  • App usage data including pages visited, session duration, and timestamps
  • Device operating system information
This application does not collect precise geographic location data from your device.
This application does not use Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies to process your personal data.
photo_camera
Camera permission

The application requests access to your device camera exclusively for scanning RFID and QR codes to record student attendance. The camera is activated only during the active scanning process.

No images, photographs, or video are captured, stored, transmitted, or shared at any time.
notifications
Push notifications

The application uses Firebase Cloud Messaging to send push notifications related to attendance records and in-app chat messages. Notifications are integral to the core functionality of this system.

You may disable notifications at any time through your device settings without affecting other application functionality.

lock
Chat system & encryption

The application includes an in-app messaging system to facilitate communication within the school. All chat messages are encrypted in transit and at rest to protect user privacy.

Chat data is accessible only to authenticated and authorized users within the school system and is not shared with or accessible by any third parties.
