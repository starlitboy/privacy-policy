Camera permission

The app requests camera access exclusively for scanning RFID and QR codes to record student attendance. The camera is only activated during an active scan session.

check_circle No images, photographs, or video are ever captured, stored, transmitted, or shared.

Push notifications

Firebase Cloud Messaging is used to send push notifications related to attendance records and incoming chat messages. Notifications are integral to core functionality.

You may disable notifications at any time through your device settings without affecting other app functionality.

lock
Chat system & encryption

campuslog includes an in-app messaging system for communication within the school. All chat messages are encrypted in transit and at rest to protect user privacy.

Messages are stored on our servers to enable conversation history and are retained for the duration of the user's account activity.

group Chat data is accessible only to authenticated and authorized users within the school system and is never shared with or accessible by any third parties.
