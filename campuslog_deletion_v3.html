⚠ Account Deletion

Delete Your CampusLog Account

CampusLog is a school-partnered platform. Because your account is linked to your institution, account deletion requires verification from your school principal before we can process the request.

⚠ Important: We cannot delete your account based on a student request alone. A signed authorization from your school principal is mandatory. This protects your data from unauthorized deletion requests.

Step-by-step deletion process

1

Student sends an email to work.dev.rik@gmail.com with the subject line:
"CampusLog Account Deletion Request – [Your Name]"

Include your registered phone number in the email
2

Get a permission letter from your Principal. Download the template below, print it, and have your school principal fill it in, sign it, and stamp it with the official school seal.

⚠ Letter must have principal's signature + school stamp
3

Attach a scanned copy of the signed permission letter to your email and send it to work.dev.rik@gmail.com.

4

We will verify with your school and process the deletion within 7 working days. You'll receive a confirmation email once done.

Confirmation sent to your registered email
